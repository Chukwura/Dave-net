About the school

    • The school has lots of social amenities like Pipe borne water, electricity and so on. The school is also one of the best in competitions, quizes and also in debates. The school has so many studious subjects like English, Mathematics, Social studies, Chemistry, Biology, Physics, Computer and so on. The school is being controlled by soldiers and prefects so that there will be peace in the school. The school has a head called the commandant. Command Day Secondary School has some room for both sports and entertainment. The school also has counselors and teachers to take care of the students and to also train them to become better people in the future.The school has so many buildings and structures and also has a field for playing football and other sports. It has about 8 buildings and about 100-200 classrooms and about 30-40 staff/subject staff rooms and with over 1008 students and 109 teachers. We have a store for the students to collect their books, uniforms, games wear and little accessories and we also have labouratorys for practice. The color of our school uniform is Light Green short sleve shirt, dark green trouser for the boys and skirt for the girls with school cap, tie, black shoe for the boys and black sandals for the girls. The school has provision shops and cafeteria for buying foods and snacks during break time and also we have Cyber Cafe for making photo copyies. The school also has a specific time for their assemblings and prayers. The school also has a religious gathering on fridays known as "PADRE HOUR" and it is held after lessons by 1pm in the afternoon. The school has a lot of staffs and soldiers and also students. The school also have the amount of money they pay for both school fees and lessons. The amount of money we pay for school fees is $80,000 and for lessons is $7000 . We also have a new computer subject called CISCO. It helps the students widen up their minds about computer and also to know the deep things about computer. The amount of money we pay for it is $3000.
