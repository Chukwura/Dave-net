COMMAND DAY SECONDARY SCHOOL ENUGU

Command Day Secondary School Enugu is a school located in Abakiliki Road Enugu, Nigeria and it was formed in 1996 by the military. Command Day Secondary School was formerly known as "Army Secondary School" before it was later changed to "Command Day Secondary School". The school has so many social amenities and also has lots of teachers, staff and students attending the school. We are so good at teaching any subject like science and art and also other subjects. We also host competitions and quizes both state and national. We help students to become great people in the future and in the world. The school is the best school in Nigeria and that is why so many parents want their wards to attend the school . Command Day Secondary School Enugu is a very good school and we will be happy that you attend our great school.
